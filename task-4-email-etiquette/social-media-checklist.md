# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting content online
- Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
- Share informative and positive content
- Be respectful and professional in communication
- Verify information before sharing

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in arguments or cyberbullying
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Do not spread fake or unverified information
